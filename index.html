> About Us

COBRA is a cybersecurity research collective dedicated to advancing offensive security knowledge through hands-on research, CTF competitions, and open-source tooling.

Our team comprises vulnerability researchers, reverse engineers, red teamers, and security analysts who collaborate to push the boundaries of what's possible in cybersecurity.

We believe in responsible disclosure, knowledge sharing, and building a stronger security community through education and collaborative research.
